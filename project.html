Camp Activities

Discover the wide range of exciting activities we offer to keep campers engaged, challenged, and having fun all summer long!

Swimming

Our supervised swimming sessions in our beautiful lake help campers build confidence in the water while having fun.

Arts & Crafts

Express creativity through various art projects, from painting and drawing to making friendship bracelets.

Sports

From soccer and basketball to volleyball and archery, our sports program offers something for every camper.

Campfire

Evening campfires with stories, songs, and s'mores create magical memories that last a lifetime.

Nature Exploration

Guided hikes and nature studies help campers connect with the environment and learn about local ecosystems.

Team Building

Fun challenges and games that promote teamwork, communication, and problem-solving skills.
